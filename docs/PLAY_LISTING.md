# Play Store listing draft — So, When?

## Short description

Turn “we should catch up” into a real plan — privately, on your phone.

## Full description

So, When? helps you pick a free time, choose a friend, and share an invitation with the apps you already use.

Friends never need So, When?, an account, or a special link. Everything stays on your device.

- Add people you want to see
- Mark when you’re usually free
- Tap a free time and create an invitation
- Track replies and mark catch-ups done
- Optional quiet local reminders

No social feed. No event discovery. No accounts.

Made by Palari Labs, Inc.
